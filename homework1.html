Bear

The bear is a large, carnivorous mammal found in various parts of the world, including North America, Europe, and Asia. Bears are known for their powerful build, large size, and shaggy fur. They are omnivores, and their diet can include berries, nuts, fish, and small mammals.

Fox

The fox is a small carnivorous mammal found in various parts of the world, including North America, Europe, and Asia. Foxes are known for their cunning and agility, and their diet can include small mammals, birds, and insects. Some species of fox are also known for their distinctive red fur.
